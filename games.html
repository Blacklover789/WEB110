Top 10 Games

Here are my favorite Games. I love these for their character setting, performances, and action.

Rank Games Title Year Released Premise of game Genre
1 The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time 1998 Link travels through time to stop Ganondorf from taking over Hyrule. Action-Adventure
2 The Last of Us 2013 Joel and Ellie must survive a journey across a post-pandemic United States. Survival Horror
3 Red Dead Redemption 2 2018 Follows outlaw Arthur Morgan and his gang during the decline of the Wild West. Action-Adventure
4 Tetris 1984 Players arrange falling blocks to form complete lines. Puzzle
5 Half-Life 2 2004 Gordon Freeman helps rid the planet of invading alien forces. First-Person Shooter(FPS)
6 The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt 2015 Geralt of Rivia searches for his adopted daughter while pursued by the Wild Hunt. Action RPG (Role-Playing Game)
7 Super Mario Bros. 1985 Mario ventures through the Mushroom Kingdom to rescue Princess Toadstool from Bowser. Platformer
8 Minecraft 2011 Players explore a 3D world, gather resources, build structures, and survive against monsters. Sandbox/Survival
9 Metal Gear Solid 1998 Solid Snake infiltrates a military base to rescue hostages and thwart a terrorist group. Stealth
10 Grand Theft Auto V 2013 Three criminals team up for heists in Los Santos. Third-Person Shooter (TPS)/Sandbox
